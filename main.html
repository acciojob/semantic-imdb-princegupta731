Summary

*Inception* follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Offered a chance to have his past crimes erased, Cobb must perform the impossible — inception, planting an idea into someone’s mind. The mission leads him through layers of dreams and reality, testing his sanity and sense of truth.
